Our mission at Petable is to help you find and adopt your dream pet, whether it’s a dog, a cat or even both, and make it as easy as possible. Here, you can browse through different shelters and their adorable residents, look at photos and read their stories. Then you can contact the shelter directly and they’ll guide you through the pet adoption process and make sure you and the animal would be a good fit. Thanks to Petable, you can explore shelters in Bulgaria all in one place, plan visits and hopefully find your new four legged friend.
